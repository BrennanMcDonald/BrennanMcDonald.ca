How It Works

Download Fashion Path
 
Download Fashion Path for free from any official App Store on your smartphone.
Add Items To Your Shopping Cart
 
Choose your favourite store and add the items that you want to your shopping cart.
Locate Your Items
 
Fashion Path uses Beacon technology with RFID tags to locate the clothes that you're looking for.
