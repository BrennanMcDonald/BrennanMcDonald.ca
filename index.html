About Me

Personal

Hi, im Brennan McDonald. Im a 3rd year student at the University of Ottawa working towards my bachelor's of science in Computer Science. When im not at school you can find me doing one of my various hobbies such as cooking, coding, gaming and playing the guitar. Outside of class I like to participate in various extra-cirruculars and clubs such as 'Ottabotics', which is the University of Ottawa's robotics team.

Technical

I have a strong background in C#, C/C++, Python, Ruby, and Java. All of which I have used for personal, school and professional projects. I have also recently been experimenting with GoLang recently and have developed a interest in using it for future projects. On top of my love for code I am also strongly suited in processor architecture and very comfortable in a linux environment. I have been running Arch Linux on my laptop for 6 years and have a strong depth of knowledge in the linux command line.

My projects have mostly been in the web and mobile development sector using frameworks such as asp.NET, Ruby on Rails and Android. However I have a strong interest in learning network and application security. I have experiments with various software suites such as the Metasploit framework and Wireshark. I have become very knowledgeable of various web based attacks such as SQLi, XSS, XSF and CLRF.
